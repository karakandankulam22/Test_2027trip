The system

How to actually track it all trip

📋 Splitwise — the running tally

Create a group ("Greece 2027") before you fly. Every time someone pays for something shared — a hostel booking, a group dinner, ferry tickets — they log it in the app, split equally or by who actually had the gyro. Splitwise keeps a running "who owes who" total the whole trip, in whatever currency, so nobody has to do mental math at 1am.

splitwise.com ↗

💵 Venmo — actually settling up

Splitwise tracks the debt; Venmo (or Zelle/Cash App) actually moves the money. Settle up at the end of each island — Athens, then Santorini, then Mykonos — instead of one giant reconciliation on the flight home.

venmo.com ↗

Suggested rhythm: rotate who books/pays for the big shared items (hostels, ferries) so it's not always the same person fronting cash — log it in Splitwise the moment you pay, and settle up on Venmo before you fall asleep that night, not "eventually."

Good to know

Tipping in Greece

🍽️ Restaurants

Not obligatory — a service charge is often already included. Rounding up or adding 5–10% for good service is appreciated, not expected.

🚕 Taxis

Round up to the nearest euro. No standard percentage expected.

🍹 Bars & beach clubs

Leaving small change or ~5% for table service at beach clubs is a nice touch, especially if you're camped out all day.
