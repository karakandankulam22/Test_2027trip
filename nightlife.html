Nightlife

From rooftop bars to the world-famous beach clubs of Mykonos — where to go and what it'll cost you.

Know before you go: Greece's legal drinking & club entry age is 18 — bring a passport as ID. Pace yourselves, drink water between rounds, and always leave and arrive together as a group.
