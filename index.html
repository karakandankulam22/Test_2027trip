The Plan

Your 7-day island-hopping route

2 nights Athens → 2 nights Santorini → 2 nights Mykonos, home on day 7.

Day 1 · Jun 11

Land in Athens (ATH)

Check into your hostel near Plaka, grab a souvlaki dinner, and walk up to a sunset view of the Acropolis.

Day 2 · Jun 12

Athens sightseeing day

Acropolis, Parthenon & Ancient Agora in the morning before the heat, Plaka & Monastiraki in the afternoon, rooftop bars in Gazi at night.

Day 3 · Jun 13

Ferry to Santorini

Early high-speed ferry from Piraeus (~4h30m). Afternoon exploring Fira, sunset in Oia.

Day 4 · Jun 14

Santorini full day

Akrotiri ruins, Red Beach, and Ammoudi Bay for fresh seafood — bar-hop Two Brothers strip in Fira at night.

Day 5 · Jun 15

Ferry to Mykonos

Morning high-speed ferry (~2h15m). Afternoon at Paradise Beach.

Day 6 · Jun 16

Mykonos full day

Optional Delos day-trip in the morning, Little Venice & windmills at golden hour, Cavo Paradiso at night.

Day 7 · Jun 17

Fly home

Morning flight/ferry connection back through Athens (ATH) for your international flight home.
