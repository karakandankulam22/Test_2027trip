Historical Sites

The must-sees on each island, with entry fees and how far they are from your hostel.

🏛️ Athens 🌋 Santorini 🏖️ Mykonos
🏛️

Athens

$21 entry

Acropolis & Parthenon

10 min from hostel

Go right at 8am opening to beat both the heat and the crowds.

$11 entry

Ancient Agora

8 min from hostel

Where Socrates actually taught — the original marketplace of Athenian democracy.

Free

Plaka & Anafiotika

2 min from hostel

Wander the whitewashed island-style alleys tucked right beneath the Acropolis.

Money-saving tip: the ~$32 combo ticket covers the Acropolis plus 6 more sites (Ancient Agora, Roman Forum, Kerameikos, Temple of Olympian Zeus and more), valid 5 days.

🌋

Santorini

$13 entry

Akrotiri

~25 min from hostel

A Bronze-Age town preserved under volcanic ash — often called the "Greek Pompeii."

$7 entry

Ancient Thera

~15 min from hostel

A clifftop ruin above Kamari with sweeping views over the Aegean.

Free

Oia Castle Ruins

~20 min from hostel

The most famous sunset-viewing spot on the island — arrive early to grab a ledge.

🏖️

Mykonos

$13 entry + $22 boat

Delos

~30 min boat from Mykonos Town

UNESCO-listed island, birthplace of Apollo in myth — boat-only, so plan a half day.

Free

Windmills of Kato Mili

~15 min from hostel

The postcard shot of Mykonos — best at golden hour.

Free

Little Venice

~15 min from hostel

Historic waterfront houses built right on the sea — grab a drink and watch the sunset.
