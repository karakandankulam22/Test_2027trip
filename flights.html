Flights & Ferries

Round-trip flight estimates into Athens (ATH), plus every ferry leg you'll need to island-hop Athens → Santorini → Mykonos and home.

Step 1

Flights into Athens (ATH)

Prices are round-trip, per person, from major U.S. hubs (NYC/Boston/DC area) for June 11–17, 2027.

OptionAirlines (example)StopsTotal travel timeEst. price / person
Budget Norse Atlantic, TAP Air Portugal, Turkish Airlines 1–2 stops ~13–17 hrs $780 – $880
Standard Delta, Lufthansa, British Airways, Aegean 1 stop ~11–13 hrs $950 – $1,100
Fastest (seasonal nonstop) Delta / Aegean JFK–ATH direct (May–Oct only) Nonstop ~9.5 hrs $1,150 – $1,350

Booking tips: Book 3–5 months out (Feb–March 2027) for the best summer fares. Tuesday/Wednesday departures run 10–15% cheaper than weekends. Set price alerts on Google Flights and Kayak now so you know what "normal" looks like before prices spike closer to June.

Step 2

Island-hopping ferries

Book via Ferryhopper.com (compares all operators) 1–2 months ahead — summer boats sell out.

Mykonos (JMK) → Athens (ATH) Flight Aegean / Olympic Air ~40 min Several daily $70 – $120

Weather heads-up: The Cyclades get strong "meltemi" summer winds that occasionally cancel high-speed ferries. Build in a buffer — don't schedule your flight home for the same day as your last ferry.

Reality check

7 travelers, one trip

✈️ Flights

Book as a group where possible — many airlines hold group seats together and some offer small group discounts for 7+ passengers.

⛴️ Ferries

Buy all 3 ferry legs together on Ferryhopper in one checkout — it'll flag any schedule conflicts automatically.

🎒 Luggage

Pack light — ferries have narrow gangways and stairs. One backpack or carry-on each makes island hopping much easier.
